Course Overview

This course offers a thorough preparation guide for the UPSC Civil Services Examination. It includes detailed lessons on General Studies, Essay writing, and Optional subjects. The course is designed to help you build a strong foundation and tackle each stage of the IAS exam with confidence.

Course Syllabus

  • General Studies Paper I: Indian Heritage and Culture, History, and Geography of the World and Society
  • General Studies Paper II: Governance, Constitution, Polity, Social Justice, and International Relations
  • General Studies Paper III: Technology, Economic Development, Bio-diversity, Environment, Security, and Disaster Management
  • General Studies Paper IV: Ethics, Integrity, and Aptitude
  • Essay Paper
  • Optional Subject (Choose one from a list of subjects)

What You'll Gain

By the end of this course, you will have a deep understanding of the IAS exam pattern, effective study strategies, and the necessary skills to tackle each paper with ease. You'll also gain access to practice tests and mock exams to gauge your preparation level.
